A.I Article Summarizer

A web-application that summarizes articles and web-pages using Open AI/GPT-4 by the user providing a url. Previous uses/summaries are also saved so they may be seen again and the user can copy previous URLs in their clipboard with a simple click. Built with React.js, TailWind CSS, Redux ToolKit, JavaScript, Vite, GPT-4

Live Link

Project Overview

To get started, all the user would need to provide is a URL to an article or web-page, that they would like the concise summary to. Place the URL into the input box and simply click enter or the submit button. The summary will be generated and displayed right below!

Multiple summaries can be generated and saved, as the user can simply click on the previous URL entries to see the summaries again. The user will also have the ability to copy the URL to their clipboard, so that that if they would like to revist the article or web-page, they have complete access to that URL!

This is for those who have trouble focusing on long articles or lenghty web-pages fully of information. Students and professionals alike can make use of tool to speed up their research and learning process and they can rest easy knowing that they have the ability to revist the article or web-page at any time. Click on the live link and try it out for yourself!
